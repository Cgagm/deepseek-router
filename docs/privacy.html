Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

1. Overview

CC China Stack is a local-first product. It runs entirely on your computer and does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. This Privacy Policy explains what data is involved and how it is handled.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or have access to:

  • Your API keys (stored as environment variables on your machine)
  • Your code, prompts, or conversations with AI providers
  • Your personal information or usage patterns
  • Any telemetry or analytics data

3. Data That Stays On Your Machine

The following data exists only on your local computer:

  • API Keys: Stored as environment variables (DEEPSEEK_API_KEY, etc.)
  • Configuration: router.config.json in ~/.deepseek-router/
  • Router Logs: Optional local logging for debugging

None of this data is sent to us or to any third party other than the AI providers you configure.

4. Third-Party AI Providers

When you use CC China Stack, your prompts and code are sent to the AI providers you have configured (DeepSeek, Tencent, Zhipu, Alibaba, ByteDance). Each provider has its own privacy policy:

  • DeepSeek Privacy Policy
  • Tencent Cloud Privacy Policy
  • Zhipu AI Privacy Policy
  • Alibaba Cloud Privacy Policy
  • ByteDance Volcengine Privacy Policy

5. Purchase Data

When you purchase CC China Stack, the payment processor (Gumroad / Paddle) collects information necessary to complete the transaction, including your email address and payment details. We receive only your email address for order fulfillment purposes. We do not receive your full payment card details.

6. Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies or any tracking technologies. The Product itself contains no tracking code.
